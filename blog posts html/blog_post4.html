Say no to single-use plastic

Everything and anything is pretty much covered in plastic

It can take 15 to 1000 years before the plastic bag you used for a couple minutes are broken down in the environment. Don't ask for a new plastic bag every time you go shopping, especially when you're just shopping a few groceries.

These plastic bags will live longer than all of us combined and way to often it ends up being digested by wildlife or tangled around marine animals.

The best option is to get yourself a reusable bag and if you really want to make a difference, contact your most used brands and corporations and encourage them to make more sustainable plastic-free choices.
